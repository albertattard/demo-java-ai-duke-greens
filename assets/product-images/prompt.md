Using the images in the [original](assets/product-images/original/) directory as style references, use the [$imagegen](~/.codex/skills/.system/imagegen/SKILL.md) skill to create a new product image `[SLUG]` from the catalogue [data](src/main/resources/data.sql) that belongs to the exact same supermarket private-label range.

Maintain the same:
- label proportions
- typography
- illustration style
- lighting
- composition
- premium European supermarket aesthetic
- cream, olive green and muted gold design language
- front-facing centred product shot
- transparent background

The only elements that should change are the packaging shape, product illustration, product name and quantity. The finished image should look as though it was designed by the same packaging designer as the reference images.

Update the [data](src/main/resources/data.sql) once the image is created.

Do not update the [current.md](docs/current.md) and ignore other uncommitted changes.
