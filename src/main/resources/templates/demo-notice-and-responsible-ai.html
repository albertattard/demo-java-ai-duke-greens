Demo notice

About This Demonstration

Duke Greens is a demonstration of an AI-assisted Java application for meal planning and virtual grocery baskets. It is not a production shopping service.

What the demo does

  • Accepts a meal-planning request.
  • Uses AI to suggest and explain possible meals.
  • Validates suggested ingredients against a curated product catalogue.
  • Creates a review-only virtual basket.

What the demo does not do

  • It does not accept payments.
  • It does not create a real order, reserve stock, arrange delivery, or create a customer account.
  • Product availability, prices, and recommendations are illustrative and may not reflect real-world conditions.

Using AI responsibly

  • AI is used to interpret requests, recommend meal ideas, and explain alternatives.
  • The application, not the AI model, remains the source of truth for catalogue products, package sizes, prices, basket contents, and simulated-order completion.
  • AI output is constrained and checked before it is used in the visitor journey.
  • The visitor explicitly confirms the simulated order; the application does not complete it autonomously.

Your information

Please do not enter personal, confidential, sensitive, health, payment, or account information.

Keep requests limited to general meal-planning preferences.
