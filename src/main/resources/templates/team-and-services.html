What we do

Discover the right opportunity

We shape use cases around a real business need, the people affected, and the evidence needed to decide whether to proceed.

Show value early

Modernise Java applications

We help evolve existing Java applications so they can support new customer needs without losing the reliability the business depends on.

Integrate AI responsibly

We connect AI where it can help people understand, decide, or create, while keeping important business decisions under application control.

Guide the architecture

Enable delivery teams

We help teams establish a practical delivery approach and leave them able to continue the work with confidence.

How we work

  • We start with collaborative workshops that bring business and technical perspectives together.
  • We deliver small, end-to-end increments so progress is visible and decisions are based on working software.
  • Your business data stays under your control; AI can assist, but it does not become the source of truth.
  • We agree measurable outcomes before treating an idea as valuable.
  • We transfer knowledge as we work, so your team can own what comes next.

A practical example

Why Duke Greens

Duke Greens shows the division of responsibility that makes an AI experience useful and trustworthy. AI understands a customer’s natural-language meal request and proposes ideas. The Java application remains in control of the catalogue, prices, basket contents, and order confirmation.

That means the experience can feel conversational without asking AI to decide what a customer can buy or what they will pay.
